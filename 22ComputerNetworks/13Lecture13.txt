~~ MAC addresses ~~

another lecture on DLL layer.

addresses are identified by MAC address in DLL.

MAC -> media access control

every machine has its own MAC address which is installed in Network Interface Card (NIC).

It is a 48 bit address. first 24 bits is unique for manufacturing company. following 24 bits are unique to your machine.

It is illegal to have more than one NIC on a computer.

In a LAN every machine knows every other machines MAC address.
